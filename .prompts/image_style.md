1. Visual Style & Atmosphere
Theme: High-tech, clean, architectural, and "airy." It should feel precise and engineered but not cluttered.
Depth: Use a mix of 2D flat UI elements for text containers and 3D isometric styles for the central hero image.
Background: Very light grey or pure white. Include a subtle, faint geometric background pattern (like a thin isometric grid or wireframe lines) to add texture without distracting from the content.

2. Layout Structure (Symmetrical 3-Column Grid)

Header: Large, centered title area with space for a 2-line subtitle.
Central Column (The Hero): A generous negative space in the distinct center of the layout dedicated to a main 3D diagram or illustration.

Left & Right Columns (Content):
These columns should be dedicated to explanatory text.
Divide these columns into modular "cards" or sections.
Left Side Alignment: Right-aligned or center-aligned towards the middle.
Right Side Alignment: Left-aligned or center-aligned towards the middle.

3. Color Palette
Primary Base: White (#FFFFFF) and Light Cool Grey (#F5F7FA).
Primary Accent (Stability): Deep Teal / Slate Blue (#1B4F55 or similar). Use this for main headers and "stable" concept icons.
Secondary Accent (Action): Vibrant Orange (#FF6B35). Use this for highlights, "kernels," active states, or key call-to-actions.
Text: Dark Charcoal (#222222) for readability (never pure black).

4. Typography
Font Family: Modern Sans-Serif (e.g., Inter, Roboto, or SF Pro Display).
Hierarchy:
Title: Bold, large (approx 40-50pt), high contrast.
Section Headers: Semi-bold, colored in the Deep Teal accent.
Body Text: Regular weight, clean, easy to read at smaller sizes.

5. Graphic Elements & Components
Text Containers: Do not use heavy borders. Use whitespace or very subtle rounded rectangles with soft drop shadows to group content.

Icons:
Create a set of placeholder slots for icons.
Style: "Spot Illustration" style—mix of flat vector shapes with slight gradients to match the 3D center.
Personas: If including people/roles, use a stylized, flat-vector avatar style (as seen in the "Reviewer/Worker" section of the reference).
Connectors: Use thin, grey lines to visually connect the side text blocks to the central hero image, implying that the text describes the center.
